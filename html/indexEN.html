Concept

We at +Cards try to solve the daily problem that you always have to rummage for your wallet in order to get, for example, your credit card or your identity card.
And with our application we created a secure possibility to carry all your documents with you on your cell phone. So they are always easy accessible.

Secruity

Our team of highly trained computer scientists guarantees complete data security.

Access to the app is via two-stage identity authentication.

In addition, the documents can be permanently backed up by a code.
